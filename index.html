OUR STORY

'MoroccanCodeQueens' is a community of young women who are passionate about coding and technology.

  • We organiz coding workshops, hackathons, and coding bootcamps, inviting women from all over Morocco to learn new skills.
  • We are here to help women enter the tech industry and pursue careers in technology.
  • We continue to inspire and empower young women to become leaders in the industry, and create a more inclusive and diverse tech community in Morocco.
  • Our mission is to change the face of technology in their country and make it accessible to all.
